## üî® How Forging Works - Complete Mechanics Guide

### The Three-Phase Forging System

**The forge roblox** features a unique skill-based forging system requiring manual dexterity:

#### Phase 1: Smelting (Pumper Control)
Control the pumper to maintain optimal temperature. Consistent timing ensures better base stats.

#### Phase 2: Pouring (Temperature Management)
Pour molten metal into the mold at the right moment. Perfect timing improves weapon quality.

#### Phase 3: Hammering (Precision Strikes)
Shape your weapon with precisely-timed hammer strikes. Better performance yields higher stats.

### Ore Combination Mechanics

Understanding how **ores the forge** system calculates final stats:

**Multiplier Averaging**
- Multipliers DON'T stack - the game uses the AVERAGE multiplier
- Example: 1x Arcane Crystal (7.5x) + 3x Stone (0.2x) = Average 2.025x
- Strategy: Keep ore multipliers in similar ranges for best results

**Trait Activation Thresholds**
- **10%+** of total ores: Trait activates at minimum effectiveness
- **20%+** of total ores: Good effectiveness
- **30%+** of total ores: OPTIMAL effectiveness (maximum trait power)

**Probability Distribution**
- Lower ore counts (3-10): Favor light weapons and armor
- Medium counts (15-30): Balanced distribution across weapon types
- Higher counts (30-51): Heavy weapons and colossal swords

**Q: What's the rarest ore in The Forge?**
**Snowite** (divine rarity, 33.3x multiplier) found in Island 3's Frostspire Expanse is the rarest and most powerful ore in the game.

**Q: Where do I find legendary ores?**
Most legendary **the forge roblox ores** are found in Island 2's Forgotten Kingdom, particularly in Volcanic Rock formations. Fireite (1/2187), Magmaite (1/3003), and Lightite (1/3333) have very low drop rates.

**Q: How do ore traits work?**

### About The Forge Weapons

**Q: What's the best weapon in The Forge?**
**Colossal Swords** (especially Dragon Slayer variant) and **Katanas** are widely considered S-tier **the forge weapons** due to their excellent DPS and range. See our **[complete weapon tier list](https://theforgecalculator.info/wiki/weapons)**.

Colossal Swords have the highest probability with **45-51 ores**. Use our **[forge calculator](https://theforgecalculator.info)** to see exact chances at different ore counts.

**Q: Do weapon variants have different stats?**
Yes! Each weapon class has multiple variants with different base damage, attack speed, and DPS. For example, Dragon Slayer (24 damage, 1.4s) differs significantly from Colossal Sword (20 damage, 1.12s).

**Q: What are the fastest weapons?**
**Daggers** have the fastest attack speeds (0.32-0.35s), but **Katanas** offer the best balance of speed (0.6-0.63s) and damage for overall DPS.

### About Forging Mechanics

**Q: Can I use more than 4 ore types?**
No, **The Forge Roblox** limits you to maximum 4 different ore types per forge, though you can use any quantity of each type within the mode's ore count limits (3-51 for weapons, 3-37 for armor).

**Q: How are multipliers calculated?**
The game uses the **AVERAGE multiplier** of all ores, not the sum. Mix 1 high ore with many low ores results in a mediocre average. For best results, use ores with similar multiplier ranges.

**Q: Does mini-game performance matter?**
Absolutely! Your performance in the three forging mini-games (smelting, pouring, hammering) significantly impacts the final stats of your equipment. Practice improves results.

**Q: How do I unlock Goblin Cave?**
Complete a 5-part quest in Island 2's Forgotten Kingdom that requires donating a total of **85,000 gold**. The cave contains exclusive legendary and mythical ores.
